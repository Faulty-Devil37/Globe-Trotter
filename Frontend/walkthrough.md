## üé® Design Philosophy
Globe Trotter uses a **Glassmorphism Design System**:
- **Depth**: Translucent cards over vibrant gradient backgrounds.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle hover effects and smooth transitions between screens.
- **Typography**: The "Outfit" font family provides a modern, readable, and premium feel.
- **Accessibility**: High-contrast text on dark backgrounds ensures readability in all lighting conditions.
